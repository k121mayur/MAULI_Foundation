About MAULI Foundation

At MAULI Foundation, we are dedicated to bringing positive changes to the lives of the underprivileged through various charitable initiatives. From supporting education to providing basic needs, we are committed to a better future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a helping hand to those in need and bring about structural changes in society by empowering marginalized communities.

Our Projects

Educational Cycle Project

Collecting unused cycles from urban areas and donating them to underprivileged children in remote areas for easier access to education.
