Cancellation and Refund Policy

At Magnifying Aspirations for Unified Life Improvement Foundation, we appreciate your support. Please read the following guidelines regarding cancellations and refunds for recurring donations.

1. Recurring Donation Cancellation

You may cancel your recurring donation at any time by contacting us or through your donor account settings. Please note that cancellation requests will take effect from the next billing cycle.

2. Refunds

Due to the nature of recurring donations, we typically do not process refunds for past contributions. However, in extenuating circumstances, please reach out to us directly, and we will consider your request on a case-by-case basis.

3. How to Cancel

  • Email us at mayur@mymauli.org.
  • Contact us via the details provided on our Contact Us page.

4. Changes to the Policy

We reserve the right to modify this cancellation and refund policy. Any changes will be updated on this page.
